Meeting negotiator:

We made this in IBM Hackathon which includs AI automations, AI agents etc,. Meeting Negotiator deals with the arrrangement of meetings between teo people.

Working:

  1. First open the app and login with your Slack account.
  2. After login select the person you want to meet.
  3. Now select your desired or your available timings to meet.
  4. After entering the details click on send.
  5. Now the app send a mail to the other via,. E-mail.
  6. The app will now the take the details from both parties and use slack AI to find a sutable timings for both the partiies.
  7. If the timing is not accepted by any one of the parties the AI will suggest a new sutabel timing.
